About Us

Founded in 2022, Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation is dedicated to transforming lives by offering quality education to children in marginalized communities. We believe that every child deserves the right to learn and grow.

Our dedicated volunteers conduct classes, organize educational drives, and create a safe learning environment to nurture the next generation.
